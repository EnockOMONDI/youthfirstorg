Who We Are

Youth First.Org is a non-profit organization that was founded to empower the youth in Africa through various programs which will enable us to have a movement of empowered and enabled youths in Africa in all walks of life. Our goal is to establish a poverty free Africa through Entrepreneurship, leadership training and Business mentorship among the youth. We take the African Youth seriously and appreciate their creativity and innovative minds and this is what Youth First.Org is tapping in its day today activities and engagement with the youth. One of the biggest problems facing Africa now is Unemployment and underemployment among the Youth.

At Youth First.Org we are very passionate about the Youth and we would like to reduce some of these issues associated with the Youth through various Interventions and high impact programs such as Youth Entrepreneurship,Leadership and Mentorship Program (YELM). We hope to reach about 5,000 youths directly through our direct various interventions and over 20,000 youths indirectly by 2030. This will enable us to create a movement of empowered and enabled youths in Africa and hence promote good living standards and poverty reduction among various families.

Our Objectives

Create alternative to formal employment among the youth between 15-25 years through entrepreneurship, leadership training and mentorship. so that they can start and build their own businesses and initiatives in their communities.

  • Build their capacities on entrepreneurial and life skills
  • Provide opportunities for the youth to get funds to put into their businesses and initiatives from loan lending institutions and business investors.
  • Enhance their experience through real life business mentorship
